Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the email we send you.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption and security measures. Your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

How do I export my data?

Premium and Pro users can export their data in PDF or Excel format from the Reports section in your dashboard.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment.

Can I use BusinessThis on mobile?

Yes! Our app is fully responsive and works great on all devices. We also have native mobile apps for iOS and Android.
